# What is Cryptocurrency?

**Category:** Crypto Basics  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Reading Time:** 5 minutes

## Overview

Cryptocurrency is a digital or virtual currency that uses cryptography for security. Unlike traditional money (like dollars or euros), cryptocurrencies exist only in digital form and operate on decentralized networks.

## Key Concepts

### 1. Decentralization
- No bank or government controls it
- Runs on blockchain technology
- Transactions verified by network participants

### 2. Blockchain Technology
- A digital ledger that records all transactions
- Cannot be altered or deleted
- Public and transparent

### 3. Popular Cryptocurrencies
- **Bitcoin (BTC)**: The first and most valuable cryptocurrency
- **Ethereum (ETH)**: Platform for smart contracts and apps
- **Others**: Thousands of "altcoins" exist

## How It Works

1. **Buying**: Use real money to purchase crypto on exchanges
2. **Storing**: Keep in digital wallets (like this simulator)
3. **Trading**: Buy low, sell high (hopefully!)
4. **Sending**: Transfer directly to others without banks

## Important Terms

- **Wallet**: Digital storage for your crypto
- **Exchange**: Platform to buy/sell crypto
- **Mining**: Process of creating new cryptocurrency
- **Volatility**: Prices change rapidly
